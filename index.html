Methodology

A Structured Path to Value

We don't sell black boxes. We build capabilities. Our modular approach ensures every investment is linked to EBITDA impact or Risk reduction.

Phase 1
Diagnose
Value Targeting & Data Readiness
  • Opportunity Scan: Mapping pain points (Grid, Assets, Customers) to AI patterns.
  • Data Audits: "Go/No-Go" assessment on SCADA/IT data quality.
  • Business Case: ROI modelling (Capex avoidance vs Opex reduction).
  • Make-or-Buy: Vendor selection vs Internal build strategy.
Phase 2
Design
Operating Model & Governance
  • Org Design: Defining roles (Data Owners, AI Leads) & decision rights.
  • Risk Framework: Setting guardrails for GenAI & Critical Infrastructure.
  • Tech Architecture: Defining the MLOps & Data Platform blueprint.
  • Partner Model: Structuring the ecosystem (Integrators, CSPs, Startups).
Phase 3
Deliver
End-to-End Build (MVP to Prod)
  • Agile Squads: Mobilising hybrid teams (CVA + Client + Tech) for delivery.
  • MVP Execution: Rapid development of models (Forecasting, Vision, NLP).
  • Process Integration: Embedding AI into legacy workflows (SAP, Maximo).
  • Validation: Rigorous testing against industrial safety standards.
Phase 4
Scale
Industrialisation & Change
  • Change Mgmt: Training field force & control room operators.
  • MLOps Scale: Automating retraining and monitoring pipelines.
  • Value Tracking: Continuous monitoring of realised gains (P&L impact).
  • In-sourcing: Transferring capabilities to client teams.
