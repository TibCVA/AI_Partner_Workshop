Our Conviction

AI is not the issue.
The interface to decisions is.

Most energy players have mature data scientists and valid pilots. Value is lost in the "Last Mile": the inability to embed AI outputs into a binding decision process with clear governance.

1 The Recurring Failure Mode

  • Pilots do not scale

    Teams build prototypes, but the "Run" organization refuses to adopt them due to lack of trust or process fit.

  • Unclear Decision Ownership

    AI outputs exist (e.g., load forecast), but CAPEX and trading decisions still rely on "gut feel" and Excel.

2 The DIDA Protocol

We decompose every process into 4 steps to make the AI role explicit and bindable.

01
Data → InsightsSanctuarise the input.
02
Insights → DecisionsDefine the rule & the owner.
03
Decisions → ActionsAutomate execution.
