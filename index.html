The Problem

AI is not the issue.
The interface to decisions is.

In Energy, AI models are commodities. Value is captured when AI is embedded end-to-end into a core process with clear decision rules, controls and governance.

The Recurring Failure Mode

  • Pilots do not scale.

    Teams build prototypes, but the business process remains unchanged and outcomes are not industrialised.

  • Decision ownership is unclear.

    AI outputs exist, yet capex, risk and operational decisions still rely on fragmented routines.

  • Value is not secured.

    Benefits are not measured and reinvested because governance is not structured.

The DIDA Protocol

Our method to decompose processes into Data, Insights, Decisions, and Actions to make AI explicit.

Step 1
Data → Insights

Clarify flows needed to run the process.

Step 2
Insights → Decisions

Define rules, controls, and accountability.

Step 3
Decisions → Actions

Embed into operating rhythms and execution.
