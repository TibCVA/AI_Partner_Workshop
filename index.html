Positioning

AI is not the issue. The interface to business decisions is.

In Energy, many AI techniques are already mature in the market. Value is captured when AI is embedded end-to-end into a core process with clear decision rules, controls and governance.

The recurring failure mode

  • Pilots do not scale. Teams build prototypes, but the business process remains unchanged and outcomes are not industrialised.
  • Decision ownership is unclear. AI outputs exist, yet capex, risk and operational decisions still rely on fragmented routines.
  • Value is not secured. Benefits are not measured and reinvested because governance is not structured.

Where CVA creates defensible value

We design the end-to-end decision process, define the control framework, and steer industrialisation with the right governance. We remain technology-neutral and mobilise execution partners when needed.

Data → Insights
Clarify data flows and what "insights" are needed to run the process
Insights → Decisions
Define decision rules, controls, exceptions and accountability
Decisions → Actions
Embed into operating rhythms, tooling and execution routines
DIDADIDA decomposes each process step into Data, Insights, Decision and Action to make the AI role explicit and implementable. is our protocol to connect processes, data and AI in a pragmatic way.
