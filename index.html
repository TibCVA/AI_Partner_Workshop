01. CORE Value Discovery & AI Use Case Radar

Content

  • Analysis of current AI and data initiatives across key processes.

Deliverables

  • A structured “AI Use Case Radar” with value and feasibility scores.
  • A short business case for each priority use case.
  • A shortlist of one or two flagship use cases per process.

Focus Programme A

Nuclear & Critical Asset Health Cockpit

At a Glance

Clients

EDF Nuclear, Framatome, Orano, Naval Group, TAQA, SEC

Core Processes

Asset strategy, life extension programmes, outage planning, capex allocation at fleet level.

01. Business problem to solve

  • Ageing nuclear and conventional fleets create a structural tension between maximising availability, ensuring safety and controlling maintenance and capex.
  • Clients have local AI initiatives, but no integrated asset health view that drives fleet-level decisions on life extension and major capex.
  • Boards and regulators increasingly expect a transparent, risk-based, data-driven justification of these decisions.

02. What the “Cockpit” would do

  • Consolidate asset health: Create a standard health index and risk score per asset/fleet, using sensor data and digital twins.
  • Support LTO and capex decisions: Simulate the impact of LTO options on risk, availability and cost at fleet level.
  • Structure outage scope: Propose optimised outage scopes and timings that balance risk reduction and lost generation.
  • Provide a COMEX-ready view: Allow senior decision makers to compare scenarios and document decisions.

03. CORE & SmartPMO Programme Structure

  • Phase 1 – CORE Diagnostic: Map current asset health and LTO decision processes. Identify where AI does not feed high-level decisions.
  • Phase 2 – Target Operating Model: Design the standard health index, decision rules and governance. Define interfaces.
  • Phase 3 – Lighthouse and replication: Run a lighthouse on one fleet segment. Use SmartPMO to track benefits and prepare replication.

Why this is a Wave 1 priority

  • It is directly portable across the EDF ecosystem, Naval’s fleets and large fleet owners like TAQA and SEC.
  • It positions CVA on a board-level, safety-critical agenda where AI is an enabler.
  • It focuses on decision design and governance (our core business) leveraging existing AI investments.

Focus Programme B

Smart Gas & Power Network Intelligence

At a Glance

Clients

GRDF, SEC, EDF CST, TAQA

Core Processes

Network operations, safety management, renewal planning, regulator dialogue.

01. Business problem to solve

  • Networks are under pressure with growing demand and integration of new sources (biomethane, PV, EV).
  • High safety/continuity requirements, with constrained capex envelopes and strong regulatory expectations.
  • Smart meters deployed and AI trialled, but no complete end-to-end chain from anomaly detection to renewal decisions.

02. What a “Network Intelligence Office” would do

  • Advanced continuous monitoring: Use AI to detect near real-time anomalies (flow, pressure, current) indicating leaks or faults.
  • Risk scoring on assets: Build probabilities and criticality scores for network sections, updated continuously.
  • Risk-based renewal planning: Prioritise replacements/reinforcements under budget constraints.
  • Steering and regulator dialogue: Provide a cockpit linking decisions to regulatory metrics.

03. CORE & SmartPMO Programme Structure

  • Phase 1 – “From data to decisions” Diagnostic: Map how smart meter/SCADA data is used. Identify breaks between detection, intervention and capex decisions.
  • Phase 2 – Network Intelligence Office Design: Define organisation, processes, decision rituals, KPIs, and interactions.
  • Phase 3 – Geographical Lighthouse & Scale-up: Launch lighthouse on a limited perimeter. Install SmartPMO governance.

Why this is a Wave 1 priority

  • It responds to a core business/regulatory challenge for GRDF and SEC, linked to their licence to operate.
  • It leverages existing smart meter/IoT investments by creating the missing structured decision process.
  • It is replicable across network clients and opens extensions like connection request triage.
